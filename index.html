About Us

Founded by Natinael Bekele, Ferrox Steel PLC is a modern steel company focused on reliable, affordable long steel products. We combine operational discipline with strong community partnerships to deliver consistent quality and on‑time supply.

Our model integrates scrap collection, production, and distribution, enabling cost control and resilient operations. We’re committed to safety, sustainability, and workforce development.
